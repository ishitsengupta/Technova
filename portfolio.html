Examine how we tackle complex architectural challenges and deliver measurable business outcomes.

FinTrack: Legacy System Modernization

Industry: Financial Services

The Problem

FinTrack relied on a monolithic 15-year-old on-premise application that suffered from frequent downtime and severely bottlenecked their capacity to onboard new enterprise clients.

The Solution

TechNova executed a phased migration to AWS using a microservices architecture. We rewrote core transaction engines in Node.js while establishing automated CI/CD pipelines.

The Result

System uptime improved to 99.99%. FinTrack reduced their infrastructure costs by 40% and accelerated new client onboarding from weeks to mere hours.

LogisTech: Route Optimization Engine

Industry: Logistics & Supply Chain

The Problem

Fleet dispatchers were manually calculating delivery routes, leading to high fuel costs, missed delivery windows, and operational inefficiencies.

The Solution

We engineered a custom AI-driven routing algorithm utilizing Python and integrated it seamlessly into a React-based fleet management dashboard deployed on Google Cloud.

The Result

Fuel expenditure dropped by 18% in the first quarter post-launch, and on-time delivery rates increased to 96%, significantly boosting overall client satisfaction.
